Decode the Solar System this National Science Week!

We all know the solar system is enormous but just how big is it really? What would it look like shrunk down to 1/773,333,000th of its size?

Georges River Libraries have created a scale model of the solar system for you to explore.

Starting at the Sun at Hurstville Library, and ending at a secret location on the western edge of the Georges River LGA, this is a planetary treasure hunt on an astronimical scale.

Jump in your spaceship, follow the clues to find all the planets this National Science Week 9-16 August!

Register on Eventbrite using the button above to receive access to the Solar Explorer kit in August, which gives you everything you need to participate in this activity. This event is a great way for families to learn STEM together and participate in a hunt that is out of this world!
